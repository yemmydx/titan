— 05 / COMPANY

The studio, the team, the thesis.

TITAN was founded in 2024 in Dubai to print homes the Gulf actually needs — climate-tuned, fast to deliver, built to last a century. Twenty-eight people, one print floor, one quiet ambition.

— OUR THESIS

The Gulf needs more homes than its trades can build. Construction here still moves at the speed of the 1980s — and the climate has gotten harder, not easier. We think the answer is fewer crews, smarter machines, and the right mineral for the heat.

— VALUES HOW WE WORK

Four things we refuse to compromise on.

01

Honest numbers.

Every claim on this site cites a test, a report, or a built project. We publish our material data, including the failures. Architecture deserves more than marketing.

02

One regional climate.

We don't ship our printer to Stockholm. We are built for the Gulf, by people who live in it. Every test we run happens at full summer temperature, not in a controlled lab.

03

Quiet handover.

A good build leaves no scars on the family. We finish on schedule, communicate every Friday, and disappear gracefully when the keys turn.

04

Long memory.

Our warranty runs three years. Our care runs longer. Every project we deliver is logged, indexed, and revisited annually for the first three years — included in price.

— BACKGROUND

Born from two industries that rarely talk.

TITAN is what happens when you put architects, robotics engineers and Gulf-based contractors in the same room and ask them to stop arguing. We started in a converted print bureau in Al Quoz with one second-hand gantry and a stubborn mix recipe.

Two years later we operate two print floors, a materials lab, a design studio, and a permitting team. Same stubborn recipe — refined three hundred times.

— TIMELINE TWO YEARS, FIFTEEN MILESTONES

A short history, told year by year.

2024

Founded in Dubai

Hadi Al-Mansouri and Yasmin El-Sayed assemble the founding team of eight in Al Quoz. First print mix poured October 2024.

2025

First permitted residential print

Marsa Pavilion 04 in Hatta becomes the first TITAN-printed building approved by Dubai Municipality. Three-day print, six-week finish.

2025

BuildOS v1 ships

Our control platform exits private beta. Three external operators trained on TITAN-X within the quarter.

2026

Bayt Organica handover

Our most ambitious residential print to date. First Dubai home with continuously-curved load-bearing walls printed on-site.

2026

Reservations open

TITAN-X v2 reservations open to builders across the GCC. First deliveries planned for 2027.

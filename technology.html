— PERFORMANCE MATERIAL SCIENCE

Built from mineral, not lumber. The numbers explain why that matters.

Every wall TITAN prints is solid, layered cementitious composite — designed for the climate of the Gulf and tested against the loads it will see. No wood frame. No drywall cavity. No organic substrate for mould or pests to colonise.

FIRE RESISTANCE

Mineral walls do not burn.

2–4hour rating

Cementitious print mix is non-combustible. Independent testing of A1-rated 3D-printed concrete walls retained over 80% of compressive strength after exposure to 450 °C, and resisted direct flame for two to four hours — long enough for safe evacuation, and far beyond the timber framing that fuels most residential fires.

SOURCE · Arch. Civ. Mech. Eng. 2025
MOISTURE & HUMIDITY

Engineered for 90% summer humidity.

<0% absorption

Inorganic walls mean no substrate for mould, mildew or termites — the three failures behind most repair claims on UAE villas. With closed-cell XPS insulation poured into print cavities, the assembly stays under 24 °C indoors during 50 °C peaks.

SOURCE · UAE Building Sciences, 2025
COMPRESSIVE STRENGTH

Stronger than the code requires.

0MPa

Our print mix tests between 28 and 43 MPa in compression — meeting or exceeding strength requirements for residential walls under UAE structural codes, while using 30–60% less concrete than conventional CMU construction.

SOURCE · NCBI Geopolymer Studies
CLIMATE & LIFESPAN

Designed for 100 years in the desert.

0% less waste

Printed monolithically, walls have no joints to fail under thermal expansion. Construction waste drops 60%, cement usage 30–60%, labour 50–80% versus standard masonry villas — proven across multiple Gulf-region pilot projects.

SOURCE · Dubai Municipality, 2024

— 02 / BUILD

From brief to handover — under one roof.

TITAN is a vertically-integrated studio. We take a piece of land and return a printed home — designed, engineered, permitted, printed, finished. One contract. One point of accountability. One quiet handover.

— THE PROCESS SIX STEPS / 90 DAYS

A timeline measured in weeks, not years.

01
Brief & Site Survey

A two-week intake. We meet on your land, walk the soil, study the orientation, and sit with you to understand how the home should live. Drone scans capture the plot to millimetre accuracy; soil samples go to the lab.

DURATION · 2 WEEKS  ·  DELIVERABLE · SITE REPORT
02
Design & Parametric Modelling

Our architects develop three options shaped to your brief and site. Once you choose one, we refine it inside BuildOS — every curve and opening becomes a precise tool path the printer can execute.

DURATION · 3–4 WEEKS  ·  DELIVERABLE · APPROVED DESIGN
03
Permits & Engineering

Structural engineers stress-test the design against UAE code for wind, seismic and thermal loads. We file everything with Dubai Municipality or the relevant emirate authority — you sign nothing without us reviewing it first.

DURATION · 4 WEEKS  ·  DELIVERABLE · BUILDING PERMIT
04
Foundation & Site Prep

Concrete foundation and underground utilities go in first, by our pre-vetted civil partners. By the time the gantry arrives, every plumbing stub and conduit is already in place — the printer doesn't wait.

DURATION · 2 WEEKS  ·  DELIVERABLE · LEVEL SLAB
05
Print & Finish

TITAN-X arrives on a flatbed, calibrates in under an hour, and begins printing. Two technicians supervise. Walls are complete in 5–7 days; roof, glazing, plumbing fit-out and finishes follow over the next 3–4 weeks.

DURATION · 5 WEEKS  ·  DELIVERABLE · WEATHERTIGHT HOUSE
06
Handover & Care

A two-day walkthrough with your project manager. You leave with documentation, warranty papers, a 24/7 service line, and an annual climate-check visit for the first three years included in price.

DURATION · 2 DAYS  ·  DELIVERABLE · KEYS
